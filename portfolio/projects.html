E-commerce website

This is a fully functional e-commerce platform designed to provide a seamless online shopping experience. The website includes dynamic product listings, a user-friendly shopping cart, and secure payment integration. Customers can browse through various categories, filter products by price, rating, and other parameters, and easily add items to their cart for checkout. The website also features user authentication for personalized accounts, order tracking, and a review system for products.
